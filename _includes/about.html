My Content Philosophy

When it comes to content creation, I believe the magic lies in the perfect blend of metric-backed strategy and human creativity. I take great pride in developing brand voices that stand out in a sea of noise and genuinely resonate with their intended audiences. The ever-shifting puzzle of SEO keeps me on my toes, and I love seeing my articles soar to the top of search results. While I firmly believe in the necessity of KPI spreadsheets and organized content calendars, I equally appreciate boundary-pushing innovation.

How AI Changed Everything

When AI started making waves in late 2022, I saw incredible potential. I realized that automating certain aspects of content creation could allow me to take on the workload of three (or more!) writers. Intrigued, I dove deep into the world of generative AI, learning everything I could. I've developed detailed style guides specifically for AI use, invested countless hours into prompt engineering, and written all kinds of automated scripts to create a streamlined process for high-quality AI-assisted content.
